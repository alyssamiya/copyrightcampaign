About the Campaign

The Copyright Awareness Campaign is an initiative dedicated to addressing the challenges creators face in protecting their work in the digital age. As digital platforms and streaming services continue to grow, the rights of artists and content creators are often overlooked, leading to issues like unfair compensation and unauthorized use of intellectual property.
Our campaign seeks to bridge this gap by raising awareness about copyright issues, educating the public on the importance of respecting creators' rights, and advocating for stronger enforcement of copyright protections. By fostering collaboration among artists, audiences, and platforms, we aim to create an environment where creativity is celebrated, protected, and fairly compensated.

Our Goals

  • Raise awareness about copyright issues on digital platforms like Spotify.
  • Advocate for fairer royalty distribution and stronger copyright enforcement.
  • Provide education and resources to help artists protect their work.
  • Encourage listeners to support creators by respecting copyright laws.
